##Ingredients

-	250g all-purpose flour
-   300g sugar
-	2 teaspoons baking powder
-	1 teaspoon baking soda
-	1/4 teaspoon salt
-	2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
-   0.25 teaspoons ground cloves
-   0.5 teaspoons ground nutmeg
-   0.5 teaspoons salt

-   3 large eggs, room temperature
-   236.59ml oil (coconut) (melted)
-	425.24g pumpkin puree
-   1 teaspoon vanilla

###Directions
1.	Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2.	Grease and flour two bread pans
3.	Sift and whisk together the dry ingredients
4.	Combine/whisk wet ingredients together in a separate mixing bowl
5.	Pour wet ingredients over dry ingredients and whisk until just combined.  Do not overmix.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
