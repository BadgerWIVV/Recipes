#Pan Fried Bao

"

* Yield: 2 doughs

## Dough Ingredients
- [ ] 280 ml warm water ~110F
- [ ] 3g sugar
- [ ] 3g yeast
- [ ] 350g AP flour

## Filling Ingredients
- [ ] 320g ground pork
- [ ] 1T cooking wine
- [ ] 1t salt
- [ ] 1t chicken powder
- [ ] 1t five (or thirteen) spice powder
- [ ] 1T light soy sauce
- [ ] 1/2T dark soy sauce
- [ ] 1T oyster sauce
- [ ] 1T sweet bean sauce
- [ ] 30ml ginger-scallion water
- [ ] 30g chopped scallions



##Instructions

* Stir fry half the ground pork until colored
* Add cooking wine, salt, chicken powder, 5/13 spice seasoning, light & dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sweet bean paste
* Once aromatic, remove from heat and combine with remaining raw pork. Add ginger/scallion water
* Stir in one direction until the meat absorbs the liquid.
* Place in freezer for 30 minutes to make wrapping easier.

* Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and water.
* Stir in one direction until no dry lumps remain
* Let rise until doubled in size

* Take frozen filling and add 30g chopped scallion and mix well
* After dough has doubled, stir a few times to release air
* Sprinkle a little dry flour on top of dough.
* Pinch off small pieces of dough, shape into wrappers, fill with meat, and seal

* Place wrapped buns in greased pan and proof for 10 minutes
* Fry over medium low heat, add water (enough to reach 1/3 up the buns bottom) and pour into pan. Cover and simmer until liquid dries.
* Enjoy or freeze
