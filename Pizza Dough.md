* Yield: 2 doughs

## Ingredients
- [ ] 320 ml warm water ~110F
- [ ] 13 sugar
- [ ] 7g yeast
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 2T (30ml) olive oil
- [ ] 450-500g AP flour

##Instructions

* Whisk the warm water, sugar, and yeast together. Let stand for 5 minutes to proof.
* Add flour, olive oil, and salf. Beat for 2 minutes on medium speed.
* Beat for an additional 5 minutes until dough passes window pane test.
* Rise the dough until doubled, roughly an hour.
* Split the dough into two
* Freeze or use
